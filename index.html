|Why Friends?

Why is FRIENDS so popular?

This is a show about love and sex and careers and a time in life when everything is possible ... about the search for commitment and security ... and the fear of commitment and security. Most of all, it's about friendship--for when you're young and single in the city, your friends are your family.

The show's enduring popularity isn't just down to a devoted fan base of nostalgic 30 and 40-somethings. It's also due to a new crop of binge-watching Gen Z-ers around the world who see themselves in its characters and situations, even though Friends premiered before some of them were even born.
